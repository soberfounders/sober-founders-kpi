Part 4: Conflicting Information (Opinions vs. Facts)
The areas where the top 1% of experts disagree—treat these as variables to test.
1. Advantage+ Shopping (ASC) for B2B
Opinion A (Ben Heath/Dara Denney): ASC is the most powerful tool Meta has built; use it for everything, including lead gen.
Opinion B (Charlie T): ASC is a "black box" that steals credit from organic and over-targets existing fans. Stick to Manual CBO for cold prospecting.
2. The "Learning Phase" Myth
Opinion A: You must stay out of the "Learning Phase" at all costs to stabilize CPMs.
Opinion B (Andrew Faris): The "Learning Phase" is a UI distraction. If the ad is making money, ignore the warning. High-volume accounts can ignore the 50-conversion-per-week rule.
3. Lead Forms vs. Landing Pages
Opinion A: Use Meta Lead Forms but add "Conditional Logic" (disqualifying people mid-form).
Opinion B: Never use Lead Forms for $1M+ niches. The quality drop is too high, and it prevents you from "owning" the pixel data on your own site.
