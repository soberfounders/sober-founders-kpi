# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-03-15*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** A real-time KPI dashboard and AI copilot for Sober Founders — tracking leads, attendance, donations, email, and outreach.
**What it does:** Aggregates data from HubSpot, Supabase, Meta Ads, Mailchimp, Google Analytics, Luma, and Zeffy into a unified dashboard with 11 views. Includes a Slack bot copilot and AI-powered outreach agents for member retention and recovery.
**Product category:** Business Intelligence / Nonprofit Operations Dashboard
**Product type:** Internal SaaS tool (React + Supabase)
**Business model:** Internal tool for Sober Founders organization — not commercially sold

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Sober Founders (nonprofit supporting entrepreneurs in recovery)
**Decision-makers:** Executive team, program managers, board of directors
**Primary use case:** Tracking organizational health across lead pipeline, event attendance, donations, and marketing
**Jobs to be done:**
- Monitor Phoenix Forum membership growth and lead qualification
- Track attendance trends and identify at-risk members
- Manage donation pipeline and donor retention
**Use cases:**
- Weekly KPI reviews and board briefings
- Automated outreach to no-shows and lapsed members
- A/B testing outreach campaigns
- Data-driven fundraising decisions

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Fragmented data across HubSpot, Zoom, Zeffy, Mailchimp, and other tools makes it impossible to get a unified view of organizational health.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Generic BI tools require extensive setup and don't understand nonprofit membership dynamics
- Manual spreadsheet tracking is error-prone and time-consuming
**What it costs them:** Missed retention opportunities, delayed follow-up with leads, inability to measure program effectiveness
**Emotional tension:** Uncertainty about whether the organization is growing or declining without real-time visibility

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Purpose-built for Sober Founders' specific KPI framework
- AI-powered outreach agents (no-show recovery, at-risk retention, winback campaigns)
- Two-tier lead qualification system ($250k and $1M+ revenue thresholds)
- Slack bot copilot for instant KPI access
**How we do it differently:** Deep integration with the organization's specific data sources and qualification rules
**Why that's better:** Zero configuration for end users — all business logic is embedded

## Brand Voice
**Tone:** Professional, data-driven, supportive
**Style:** Direct, clear, action-oriented
**Personality:** Trustworthy, analytical, mission-focused
