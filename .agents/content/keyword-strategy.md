**Sober Founders' unique advantage:** No competitor addresses recovery. The "sober entrepreneur" niche is uncontested. Category keywords like "peer advisory group" and "entrepreneur mastermind" are high-competition, but adding a recovery angle makes them low-competition and highly specific.

## Keyword Strategy Notes

- **No competitor targets recovery.** Every "recovery + business" keyword is essentially uncontested. This is Sober Founders' biggest strategic advantage.
- **Comparison content is high-converting.** EO, YPO, and Vistage all compete for the same category terms. Sober Founders can draft off their brand searches.
- **Question-based keywords match AI search behavior.** Long-tail questions (rows 30-40) are exactly how people prompt ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. Owning these answers = AI citation.
- **GSC data needed.** This strategy is based on competitor analysis. Once GSC access is working, validate against actual impression/click data to prioritize.
