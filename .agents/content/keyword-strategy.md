# Sober Founders — Keyword Strategy

*Last updated: 2026-03-17*
*Sources: Competitor analysis, Semrush domain data, published keyword research, Google Trends*

---

## Audience Filter

Every keyword MUST target the intersection of **entrepreneur/business owner** AND **recovery/addiction**. Content and meta descriptions must make clear this is for people in recovery who run businesses — not "sober curious" casual drinkers, not general mental health, not rehab seekers.

**Sober Founders' unique advantage:** No competitor addresses recovery. The "sober entrepreneur" niche is uncontested. Category keywords like "peer advisory group" and "entrepreneur mastermind" are high-competition, but adding a recovery angle makes them low-competition and highly specific.

---

## Search Volume Reality

Vistage alone drives ~20K branded searches/month and 55K organic visits/month (Semrush confirmed). The recovery + business niche keywords are low volume individually (50-500/mo each) but completely uncontested. The strategy is to capture high-volume adjacent traffic and funnel it to the community.

## Keyword Strategy Notes

- **No competitor targets recovery.** Every "recovery + business" keyword is essentially uncontested. This is Sober Founders' biggest strategic advantage.
- **The content pyramid is essential.** Niche keywords alone won't drive meaningful traffic. High-volume gateway content (high-functioning alcoholic, sobriety success stories) brings in the right audience at scale, then internal links funnel them to the community.
- **All meta descriptions must signal BOTH recovery AND entrepreneurship.** This filters out non-target visitors and improves CTR for the right audience.
- **Comparison content is high-converting but SEO-only.** EO, YPO, and Vistage comparison pages work great for organic search but cannot be used in Ad Grant campaigns.
- **Question-based keywords match AI search behavior.** Long-tail questions are exactly how people prompt ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. Owning these answers = AI citation.
- **Seasonal spikes are real.** Dry January and Sober October drive 5-10x traffic spikes on sobriety keywords. Plan content 2-4 weeks before each.
