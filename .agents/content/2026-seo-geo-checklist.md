# 2026 SEO + GEO Blog Publishing Checklist

*Last updated: 2026-03-16*

In 2026, the SEO landscape has shifted from "Ranking Links" to "Owning the Answer." This checklist merges traditional technical excellence with GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) requirements to ensure the Sober Founders brand is cited by LLMs like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity.

---

## Phase 1: GEO & AI-Ready Content

Focus: Optimizing for LLM extraction and Generative Engine visibility.

- [ ] **BLUF Method:** Place the Bottom Line Up Front. Provide a direct, 40–60 word answer to the primary query in the first paragraph.
- [ ] **Question-Based H2s:** Structure subheaders as natural language questions (e.g., "How do I [X]?") to match how users prompt AI assistants.
- [ ] **Information Gain (IG) Audit:** Ensure every page contains unique data, proprietary case studies, or expert quotes. If the content only summarizes existing web data, LLMs will ignore it.
- [ ] **Modular "Chunking":** Use bulleted lists, tables, and short paragraphs. AI engines prioritize "extractable" structured blocks over rambling narratives.
- [ ] **Fact-Density Reinforcement:** Add specific statistics and benchmarks. AI agents look for "grounding truths" to increase the confidence score of their generated answers.
- [ ] **Citation-Focused PR:** Actively pursue mentions on high-authority "Seed Sites" (Reddit, Wikipedia, industry news). LLMs use these as primary training and citation sources.

## Phase 2: Technical Entity Management

Focus: Building a machine-readable site structure.

- [ ] **INP (Interaction to Next Paint) Supremacy:** Ensure INP score is <200ms. Speed is now a "Retrieval Qualifier" — if the site is slow, it is deemed untrustworthy by bots.
- [ ] **Advanced Schema Deployment:** Beyond basic Article schema, implement:
  - Organization (to link brand to specific entities)
  - Person (for author E-E-A-T signals)
  - FAQ and HowTo (to provide direct injection points for AI Overviews)
- [ ] **Robots.txt "Bot Governance":** Configure specific permissions. Allow OAI-SearchBot and Google-Extended for citations, while blocking non-beneficial training scrapers.
- [ ] **IndexNow Integration:** Use the "Push Protocol" (via Cloudflare or Akamai) to notify search engines of content updates instantly. Waiting for a crawl is a 2024 tactic.
- [ ] **Mobile-First Verification:** Perform 100% of technical audits using a Smartphone User Agent. Desktop-only issues are officially irrelevant for 2026 ranking.

## Phase 3: Authority & Trust (E-E-A-T)

Focus: Proving human expertise in an AI-saturated web.

- [ ] **Verified Author Bios:** Link author profiles to LinkedIn/X and include "Proof of Work" (e.g., "10+ years in [Industry]").
- [ ] **Freshness Cycle:** Refresh high-value content every 60–90 days. LLMs prioritize "Temporal Relevance" — outdated data is a disqualifier.
- [ ] **Entity Salience:** Use consistent brand naming across the web. Ensure Sober Founders is always described with the same core "Entity" keywords (e.g., "nonprofit for sober entrepreneurs," "Phoenix Forum") to help AI map the brand accurately.
- [ ] **Zero-Click Value Assets:** Embed original diagrams, code snippets, charts, templates, or checklists. These "visual facts" are highly likely to be reused in AI-generated answers.

## 2026 Success Metrics

Traditional "Rankings" are now a secondary metric. Track these instead:

| Metric | What It Measures | How to Track |
|--------|-----------------|--------------|
| **AI Share of Voice (SoV)** | How often is Sober Founders cited in ChatGPT/Gemini for our niche? | Monthly manual audit of top 20 queries |
| **Citation Rate** | What percentage of top pages are sourced in AI Overviews? | Google Search Console + manual checks |
| **Sentiment Score** | How does the AI describe the brand? (Expert, Reliable, etc.) | Query brand name in ChatGPT/Perplexity monthly |
