What is Sober Founders?

Sober Founders is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit community for entrepreneurs in recovery from addiction. We provide free weekly mastermind sessions, peer support, and the Phoenix Forum—an exclusive peer advisory board for founders with $1M+ in annual revenue and 1+ year of sobriety.

Our members represent over $1 billion in combined revenue across technology, real estate, healthcare, and professional services. Founded in 2020, we are the largest peer community at the intersection of entrepreneurship and recovery.
